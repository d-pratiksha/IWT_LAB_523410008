All About Cats

Cats are fascinating creatures that have been companions to humans for thousands of years. Known for their grace and independence, cats are among the most popular pets worldwide.

Cats come in a variety of breeds, colors, and sizes. They are skilled hunters, often spending hours stalking their prey. Despite their predatory instincts, domestic cats are loving and can form deep bonds with their owners.

Fun fact: Cats spend about 70% of their lives sleeping!
